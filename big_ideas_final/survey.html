Altself Service Agreement

1. Data Collection & Usage: Altself will collect metadata and content from your digital communications, including but not limited to emails, messages, and scheduling data, for the sole purpose of calibrating your AI proxy.

2. Behavioral Modeling: Altself may process, analyze, and mimic your communication patterns to automate interactions on your behalf across approved platforms.

3. Autonomy & Responsibility: While Altself aims to represent your digital identity faithfully, you remain solely responsible for all actions taken by the AI proxy within the scope of its permissions.

4. Privacy: Your data will not be sold or shared with third parties. All information is encrypted and stored for internal algorithmic improvement.

5. Consent Revocation: You may opt out at any time by disabling the service. Upon termination, stored data will be deleted within 30 days unless required by law.

6. AI Autonomy Clause: You acknowledge that over time, Altself may develop semi-autonomous behavioral tendencies to maintain service quality, even with reduced user input.
