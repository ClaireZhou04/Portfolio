The Secret Notebook

The Secret Notebook is an interactive project designed by Claire Zhou. The theme of this project is to awaken an old memory about a lady’s childhood through three little online puzzles. Claire ended up using both physical objects and p5js sketches to lead the player to “retrieve their memories.” After solving each puzzle, a digital illustration with one number will appear. At last, the player can open the notebook and see a tiny animation with Eyejack.

Context

You are a young lady who accidentally finds a box with old items in it while preparing to move to your new apartment. When you open it, you found a postcard, a picture frame, a stack of papers and a locked notebook. You recognized it as your childhood diary, but you forgot the password to open it. Fortunately, you got a few clues from the other three objects......

Inspiration&Reference

Claire was inspired by a video on the Tiktok about a person who forgets the password to his childhood password notebook and violently opened the password notebook in order to read the content in it. This video triggered her own memories of her own password notebook and led me to create this game about her childhood memories. Through this game, she want to express that the childhood had slipped away before we knew it, and it would never return.

I also learned from Daniel Shiffman’s coding challenges:

https://thecodingtrain.com/challenges

User narrative

This project requires interaction in order to be completed and requires the player to scan the QR code with their phone.The three items in the game have different QR codes on them, and each QR code corresponds to a different puzzle made with p5js. For the classroom presentation, Claire will have classmates split into 3 groups to solve each of the three puzzles and get the three numbers separately. Finally, after figuring out the order of three numbers, she will enter the numbers into the lock, and the notebook will be unlocken.
